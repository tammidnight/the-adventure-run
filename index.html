The Adventure Run

Goal of the Game

You are a little red panda, which is stranded in the desert. Luckily you have your skateboard with you.
The goal is to bring the little red panda back to all his friends.

Instructions

Avoid the rocks and meteors with jumping and ducking.
To get healthy back to your friends the raspberry and bamboo might help you.
Move with Arrow Up and Arrow Down.
While in game you can press 'm' to get some fancy music or mute it again.
